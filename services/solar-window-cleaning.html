The Service

What our Solar Panel and Window Cleaning includes.

Dust, pollen, bird droppings and hard-water spots don't just look bad — on solar panels they block sunlight and can measurably cut your energy production. We clean panels using a gentle, purified-water method that lifts grime without harsh chemicals or abrasive tools that could scratch the glass or void your warranty.

The same pure-water, spot-free approach gives your windows a crystal-clear, streak-free finish inside and out. Whether it's a full home of windows or a roof array of panels, we get them gleaming — safely and fully insured.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions.

Does cleaning really improve solar panel output?

Yes. Dirty panels can lose a noticeable percentage of their output, especially after pollen season or a dry spell. Clean panels absorb more sunlight, so regular cleaning often pays for itself in recovered production.

Will you scratch my panels or windows?

No. We use soft brushes and purified water rather than abrasive pads or harsh chemicals, which protects the glass and keeps your panel warranty intact.

How often should panels and windows be cleaned?

For most Central Ohio homes, once or twice a year keeps panels performing and glass clear. Homes near fields, trees or busy roads may benefit from more frequent visits.
